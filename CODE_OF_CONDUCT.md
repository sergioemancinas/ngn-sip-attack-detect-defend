# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by
opening a GitHub issue, or privately through the repository's **Security** tab
("Report a vulnerability") for sensitive matters. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary
and appropriate to the circumstances.
